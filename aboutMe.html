Introduction

I am a Mathematical Biologist and Applied Mathematician, with interests in broad areas, tissue dynamics, immune system dynamics, and developing efficient simulation methods.

Academic Positions

  • July 2025 – Present

    Research Fellow, The University of Melbourne

    I am currently a Research Fellow at The University of Melbourne, working with Prof. James Osborne on developing realistic individual based models in the context of tissue dynamics.

  • January 2024 – April 2025

    Postdoctoral Research Fellow, The University of Sydney

    I was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at The University of Sydney, with Prof. Peter Kim, Prof. Federico Frascoli (Swinburne University of Technology) and A/Prof. Robyn Araujo (The University of Melbourne), focusing on mathematical modelling of immune responses and the interactions between T cells and Dendritic cells.

  • July 2023 – December 2023

    Research Associate

    I had a short Research Associate position at The University of Melbourne, working with Prof. Jennifer Flegg on developing hybrid stochastic-deterministic compartmental systems.
